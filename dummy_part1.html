Data Science Report: Analysis of Widget Sales (Part 1/2)

Section 1: Introduction

This report details the analysis of widget sales data from the last fiscal year. The primary goal is to understand key drivers of sales and provide actionable insights. This first part covers data loading, initial cleaning, and exploratory data analysis.

1.1 Data Source

The data was extracted from the company's internal sales database, covering the period from January 1, 2024, to December 31, 2024.

1.2 Initial Data Cleaning

Preliminary data cleaning involved:

  • Removing entries with missing sales figures.
  • Standardizing regional codes.
  • Converting date strings to datetime objects.
